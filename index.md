# ZeroSkid: Preventing Wet-Road Accidents with AI

ZeroSkid is an assistive driving system that uses computer vision and deep learning to detect wet pavement in real time. Wet roads cause thousands of preventable crashes every year. Our goal is to give drivers early warnings before traction is lost.
