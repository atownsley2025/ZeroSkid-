# Key Takeaways

- Wet roads are a major cause of avoidable crashes.
- AlexNet provides fast, accurate detection of slippery pavement.
- ZeroSkid demonstrates real-time, proactive accident prevention.
- Future work includes nighttime detection, black ice detection, and mobile deployment.
